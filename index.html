Workplace Policies

To manage the organization with prime focus on productivity and least management overhead, EAZYSTAR Systems Inc and its group companies follow:

  • Learning first: everyone learns first and then works on it instead of learning while doing it, reducing errors that can hurt productivity, timelines, and customer satisfaction. This also prevents low motivation caused by repeated failures.
  • No negotiation during new hire selections: ensures fair compensation for everyone and equal opportunities within the organization.
  • No retention after resignation: encourages employees to raise concerns about compensation or work as soon as they are felt.
  • Self-appraisal: everyone is compensated according to performance delivered. Team members are encouraged to continuously self-review progress and email self-appraisals to self-appraisals@eazystar.us with details about improvements achieved since the last appraisal.

Self appraisal email format:

Date of Last appraisal-
Current compensation:
New learnings since last appraisal:
Other key achievements:

If the last appraisal is pending for implementation for financial reasons, the next self appraisal can still be submitted. Once the company has the budget, the latest approved self-appraisal will go into effect.

  • No smoking: ensures everyone feels safe in the workplace.
  • No notice period: so employees love working with the company and can move on when their mind changes.
  • Flexible leaves: employees can manage work-life balance without stress. Unavailability must be communicated through OOO/Break so the team can mitigate risks. Medical and exam leaves are paid unless within the first 30 days of joining. Other leaves are paid if within a reasonable limit. Abuse of this flexibility may lead to separation after review by a randomly chosen 5-member committee.
  • Self-payroll.
  • Availability between 2-10 pm.
  • PPP: In PPP, work is planned, progress is tracked, and problems are highlighted to facilitate timely resolution. PPPs also help reference data during self-appraisal submission and review.

PPP format:

Date
Plan
Progress
Problems

Steps to draft the PPP:

  • Record today's progress with each item in a new line.
  • Highlight any problems faced in the problems column.
  • Include links to GitHub PRs, deployment URLs, LinkedIn posts, YouTube videos, or any relevant progress/problem items.
  • Plan the tasks for tomorrow and create GitHub issues with links in the Plan column.
